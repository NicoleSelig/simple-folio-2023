About Me.

Hello! My name is Nicole and I enjoy creating digital experiences. My interest in programming started back in 1999 when I decided to try editing custom Xanga themes — turns out hacking together a custom blog taught me a lot about HTML & CSS! I chose to work in Veterinary Medicine for a period of time, but couldn't fight the pull to software engineering.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at an consulting agency, a university IT help-desk, a pet store, and a research lab. My main focus these days is implementing enterprise DevOps solutions at SEP.
